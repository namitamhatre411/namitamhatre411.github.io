Assignment 1 : Doodles

Collaborative Mural Painting :
• To create a collaborative mural painting experience for multiple people using spray cans, where the painting medium (wall) and the spray cans will be marked with AR markers; multiple cameras will point at the wall where people paint, and pick up on the positions of the cans w.r.t to the wall and the computer coordinates the positions of the cans (people/painters) and directs them where to/not to spray-paint.
• Software Technologies: Computer Vision, Visualization, Graphics, Database, Networking, etc.
• Hardware Technologies: Augmented spray cans, Tracking System (AR Markers), Embedded communication, Arduinos
